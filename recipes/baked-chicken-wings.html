Baked Chicken Wings


Description

A crispy, easy-to-make recipe which can be prepared in a little more than an hour. This dish goes great with rice and gravy.


Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 10 chicken wings
